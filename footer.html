About us

Football in India is an Instagram page started with the primary aim to promote football in the country. This website is just another step to boost our aim. With this website, we aim to make all our followers and all those who follow Indian Football engaged with games and other fun activities. Currently, we don't have any plan to publish News', Articles or reports on this website as there are lots of websites which provides reliable informations. But, We can assure you that you will have tons of fun by visiting this website as we plan to update the website with interesting Fun Activities and Games periodically. You can also be part of our campaign by sharing your ideas to us that can be implemented here. You may mail us your ideas which we can assure that, will not go unheard.
